1. Introduction

Welcome to the Doctor Schedule App ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We may collect the following personal information:

  • Account Information: Username, email address, password (encrypted)

2.2 Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information when you use our app:

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

8. Data Deletion and Account Removal

How to Request Data Deletion:

Complete Account Deletion:

To request complete deletion of your account and all associated data:

  1. Contact us directly at zub165@yahoo.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request"
  2. Provide your username and email address for verification
  3. Specify that you want to delete your account and all associated data
  4. We will process your request within 30 days

Partial Data Deletion:

To request deletion of specific data without deleting your entire account:

  1. Contact us at zub165@yahoo.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request"
  2. Specify which data you want to delete (e.g., old schedules, appointment history)
  3. We will process your request within 14 days

Important Information:

  • Data Types Deleted: All personal information, medical records, schedules, and appointment data associated with your account
  • Data Types Retained: Anonymous usage statistics and data required for legal compliance
  • Retention Period: Data will be permanently deleted within 30 days of your request
  • Verification Required: We may require identity verification before processing deletion requests
