Privacy Policy for Doctors Schedule

Effective date: September 16, 2025

Doctors Schedule (“we,” “our,” or “us”) operates as a scheduling application for doctors and patients. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: When you register, we may collect your name, email address, and other profile details.
  • Usage Data: We may collect non-identifiable data such as device type, operating system, and in-app activity for analytics and app improvement.
  • Scheduling Data: Appointment times, doctor availability, and patient booking information that you enter into the app.

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To provide scheduling and reminder functionality.
  • To improve app performance and user experience.
  • To communicate important updates and notifications.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share limited data only in these cases:

  • With service providers (e.g., hosting or analytics tools).
  • If required by law, regulation, or legal process.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. User Rights

You may:

  • Request access or correction of your data.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Control notification and communication preferences.

6. Children’s Privacy

Doctors Schedule is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted here with a revised “Effective Date.”
